"My Eyes"

By: Travis Scott

"Look in my eyes, tell me a tale Do you see the road, the map to my soul?..."

"MY EYES" by Travis Scott. The song contrasts smooth, introspective vocals with introspective lyrics questioning pain, shame, and personal struggles. It captures a blend of vulnerability and internal tension, often linked to the highs and lows of success and excess. Sampha's soulful chorus adds a haunting quality, enhancing the contemplative mood.
